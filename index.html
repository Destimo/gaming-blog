About

My name is Destiny and I am 23 years old. I write about a variety of gaming-related subjects, with my posts taking the form of reviews, countdowns or longer articles.

I've been a console gamer since I was a young child - my console experience ranges from the original PlayStation to Xbox 360 to the Nintendo Switch. Gaming is truly one of my passions. I have also been a PC gamer since November of 2023, and write a series based on my experiences with switching to this platform.

I'm into a variety of franchises, including: Spyro, The Legend of Zelda, Sonic the Hedgehog, Hades, Doom and The Elder Scrolls. I also love finding indie gems. My favourites this year have been Frogun, Duck Detective: The Secret Salami, Palworld and Bugsnax. I would include the game I'm currently playing, but I tend to juggle a lot of games at once - I'm a very sporadic gamer!

Gaming History

Growing up, I was exposed to a lot of games consoles. This is largely due to the fact I have an older sister, meaning she had consoles that she handed down to me. Not only this, but I took an interest myself and bought current consoles. This means that I have a unique blend of gaming experience; I grew up with the same consoles as you'd expect from Gen Z - Xbox 360 etc., but I also could go and pick up my sister's GameBoy Advance. Because of this, I have an appreciation for the older games and consoles, while also staying up-to-date on current releases. Indeed, growing up with 'older' games (games that are before my generation) meant that I became curious about trying the classics.

This is something that has stayed with me and I feel it has helped to keep my expectations reasonable when it comes to gaming. I am able to look at these games - which can sometimes be quite barebones - and see their appeal. I've also experienced a variety of consoles and would say I have a strong understanding of their capabilities and limitations, and how these affect their games.
